Second Trimester

What to do
  1. For lower back pain, regular exercise is recommended.
  2. To sleep more soundly, roll to your left side. Side sleeping also improves blood flow to your baby.
  3. Change positions often when you sit or stand. This will ease stress on your back.

Third Trimester

What to do
  1. Get plenty of rest and sleep.
  2. Keep your teeth and gums healthy.
  3. Increase your iron by eating beef, chicken, spinach, and beans.
  4. Talk to your doctor about what you'll do when those first contractions hit.
